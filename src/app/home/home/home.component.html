Dev Jam 2018 Capstone Project

The application that MAX Technical training had us pursue was creating a functional Purchase Request System (PRS). The purpose of the project was to provide everyone a preview of a practical application with real world qualities. Our job was to build a serviceable app that included different technologies ranging from database work all the way to styling a web page. Thus, teaching us the use and importance of being a full stack developer.

The PRS Application provides a user with the ability to request certain items that he or she may need from their company. Every request made by the user can contain multiple products, and different quantities.
All users must sign into a login page where they are identified as a reviewer or as an administrator. From there, they have the ability to create a purchase request, add products, delete products, and change them accordingly. The purchase request is then looked at by a reviewer who makes the decision to accept or reject it.
